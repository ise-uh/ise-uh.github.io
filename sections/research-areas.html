Research Areas

Machine Learning & Artificial Intelligence

Development of novel machine learning algorithms and their applications in real-world scenarios. Focus areas include:

  • Deep Learning and Neural Networks
  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Computer Vision and Image Processing
  • Natural Language Processing

Robotics and Automation

Design and development of intelligent robotic systems for various applications. Key research includes:

  • Robot Learning and Control
  • Human-Robot Interaction
  • Autonomous Navigation
  • Multi-Robot Systems

Intelligent Systems Engineering

Integration of AI and machine learning in complex engineering systems. Research focuses on:

  • Smart Manufacturing Systems
  • Industrial IoT Applications
  • Predictive Maintenance
  • System Optimization

Data Analytics and Processing

Advanced data analysis techniques for complex systems and processes. Areas of focus include:

  • Big Data Analytics
  • Real-time Data Processing
  • Statistical Analysis
  • Pattern Recognition
